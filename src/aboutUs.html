  • Dennis, Agalli

  • President, Founder

  • Dennis Donato Agalli is a Miami-based hospitality owner and operator and real estate professional. He has more than a decade of experience that has led to his current initiative: creating affordable housing solutions for American families.

    Born in Europe, Agalli grew up in New York, after he graduated from Stivers High School of the Arts in Dayton, Ohio. Agalli's educational background includes Manhattan College in Riverdale, NY where he earned a BBA in Finance and a minor in Economics.

    Agalli's 2022 vision is creating affordable housing. Miamians struggling with high rents need access to solutions that keep them in their homes. Through the National Affordable Housing Association, Agalli is creating real estate solutions that help low income families and their children avoid displacement as rents rise.


  • Giulia, Bevilacqua

  • Vice President

  • “I stand up for what is right.”
    As someone who has had the opportunity to help others, Giulia has managed to acquire a diverse, educational background and work experience. Through her work in National Affordable Housing Association, Giulia is creating solutions that ensure that others are able to have an impactful and successful journey into American society at large.

    Growing up in Italy, Giulia was raised having spoken multiple languages. She graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Languages. She is fluent to business level proficiency in Italian, Spanish, and English.

    Through her work in the community through activism, advocacy, and immigration law, she makes sure that immigrants, permanent residents, and refugees are all protected under the Fair Housing Laws of the United States of America.
